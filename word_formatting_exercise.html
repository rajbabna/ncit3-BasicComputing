Type or paste the title below. Format it as: Times New Roman, 18 pt, Bold, Centred.
For the body paragraph that follows, use Arial 12 pt and set line spacing to Double.

Down the Rabbit Hole

Alice sat near the riverbank feeling quite bored, when suddenly a white rabbit wearing a tiny waistcoat rushed past muttering urgently. She leapt to her feet and chased it straight to a dark hole beneath an old oak tree. Without a single sensible thought she tumbled in, falling slowly through shelves of jam jars, dusty clocks, and curious paintings until she landed softly far below in a long, dimly lit corridor lined with locked wooden doors of every possible size.

Task T2 — Paragraph Formatting
Apply the following to the paragraph below:
  • Alignment: Justified
  • First-line indent: 1.25 cm
  • Spacing before paragraph: 6 pt  |  Spacing after: 12 pt
  • Line spacing: 1.5 lines

On a small glass table she discovered a golden key and a bottle labelled Drink Me in neat handwriting. After much hesitation Alice drank it, shrinking rapidly until the great doors towered above her. She soon found a tiny cake marked Eat Me and nibbled carefully, hoping it might restore her height. Nothing about this underground world followed the ordinary rules she had been taught, and she began to wonder whether sensible rules had ever truly mattered at all.

The Queen of Hearts strode across the croquet lawn shouting orders at everyone in sight. Her soldiers, who were ordinary playing cards bent awkwardly into hoops, tried their best to remain still. Alice watched the game proceed with growing confusion, for no rule seemed fixed and the Queen changed them whenever it suited her.
